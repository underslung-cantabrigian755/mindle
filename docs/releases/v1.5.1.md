## 🔍 Quick Look fixes

v1.5.0 shipped the Quick Look extension but it stalled or fell back to the system previewer on most setups. Two fixes:

- **Drop mermaid from the Quick Look bundle.** Sandboxed extensions can't always load mermaid's runtime cleanly — that was the source of the Tahoe spinner. Diagrams aren't critical for a Spacebar preview anyway. The full reader (in the app proper) still has mermaid.
- **Hard 2-second timeout on the preview's completion handler.** Belt-and-suspenders against any JS-side hang — whatever the WebView has painted at that point becomes the preview, so you never see a forever-spinner.
